BA2 Manager for Mod Organizer 2 - The BA2 Management Tool

____________________

Take control of your BA2 files, don't let them keep you from using those new mods that you want to try.


BA2 Manager is a modern, standalone tool designed to help you manage your Fallout 4 archives effortlessly. It takes the guesswork out of the BA2 limit and helps keep your load order stable.

Fallout 4 has a hard engine limit: it can only load around 500 BA2 archive files.  If you go over this limit, your game will crash on startup or cause graphical corruption..

BA2 Manager scans your load order, counts your BA2s, and lets you safely extract mod archives into loose files. This lowers your BA2 count while keeping the mod active in your game. ### üõ†Ô∏è How to Use
Install Fallout 4, the FO4 Creation Kit, and set up Mod Organizer 2 to manage it (Links in the Requirements above).
Download the latest version of BA2 Manager from the Files tab and extract it. You can place it anywhere, but inside your MO2 folder is a good spot - it will auto-detect MO2 and Fallout 4 if you do.
Run ba2-manager.exe. Make sure MO2 is not running while using it, they can step on one another and cause problems.
Go to the Settings tab and make sure your paths are correct to your MO2 Mods folder and Archive2.exe and hit save settings.
Check the BA2 Information tab to see your current count.
Go to Manage Mod BA2s, check the mods you want to extract, and click Apply Changes.
Watch your BA2 count drop and your game stability rise!
