3.  Go to the **Settings** tab and make sure your paths are correct (MO2 Mods folder, Fallout 4 folder, and `Archive2.exe`).
4.  Check the **BA2 Information** tab to see your current count.
5.  Go to **Manage Mod BA2s**, check the mods you want to extract, and click **Apply Changes**.
6.  Watch your BA2 count drop and your game stability rise!
